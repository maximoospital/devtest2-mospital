The SCIENCE behind ADVANCED  MENOPAUSE SUPPORT:

Hot Flashes

The Biology

As you enter menopause, there's a natural decline in your estrogen levels. Estrogen influences how the body regulates temperature which is why its decline results in hot flashes, one of the most common symptoms. 

How This Helps

Using Rheum rhaponticum (ERr 731®) can mimic natural estrogen production in your body. These phytoestrogens can help reduce the frequency and intensity of these flashes and night sweats.

Mood Swings

The Biology

Estrogen impacts your mood and cognitive functions. For example, proper levels of estrogen can minimize your mood swings and brain fog, improving your emotional stability and mental clarity during menopause.

How This Helps

Ingredients, such as ashwagandha and black cohosh, work in harmony with ERr 731® to further support hormonal balance and promote relaxation, regulate mood, and support reproductive health.

Metabolism

The Biology

Hormonal changes during menopause can make it harder for women to maintain a healthy weight, leading to frustration and potential health risks. 

How This Helps

Green tea extract and chromium in Advanced Menopause Support can support healthy metabolism and help manage weight, results might be even better when combined with a balanced diet and regular exercise.
